Marketplace & Customers Foundations

Needs → Wants → Demands. Market offerings deliver value; avoid marketing myopia by focusing on benefits/experiences.

Key concepts
  • Needs: states of felt deprivation
  • Wants: needs shaped by culture/personality
  • Demands: wants + buying power
  • Market offering: products/services/experiences
  • Exchange & relationships
